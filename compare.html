Frequently Asked Questions

Q
How do I compare banks on BankSize?
Select up to 3 banks from the dropdown menus, then click 'Compare Banks' to see their combined assets, employees, and what they could achieve together.
Q
What metrics are used to compare banks?
We compare banks using total assets, market capitalization, number of employees, country of origin, and year founded. We also show real-world comparisons like years to end world hunger.
Q
Which banks can I compare?
You can compare any of the 34 largest banks in the world, including ICBC, JPMorgan Chase, Bank of America, HSBC, and major banks from China, Europe, Japan, and other regions.
