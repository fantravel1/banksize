HSBC Total Assets - Europe's Largest Bank

HSBC Holdings plc is one of the world's largest banking and financial services organizations, with approximately $3.0 trillion in total assets. This makes HSBC the largest bank in Europe and one of the top 10 largest banks globally by total assets.

HSBC's History and Growth

HSBC was founded in 1865 as the Hongkong and Shanghai Banking Corporation to finance trade between Europe and Asia. Key milestones include:

  • 1865: Founded in Hong Kong to finance growing trade between Europe and Asia
  • 1992: Acquired Midland Bank, becoming UK's largest bank
  • 1999: Became HSBC Holdings plc, reflecting global presence
  • 2024: Maintains position as Europe's largest bank with $3.0T in assets
