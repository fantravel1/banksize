About BankSize

BankSize started with a simple question: how can we make sense of numbers in the trillions? Traditional articles list banks by assets and rankings, but those figures are abstract. By comparing bank assets to real‑world items – from iPhones to entire countries – we help you understand just how massive these institutions are. All figures are estimates based on recent financial reports and publicly available data.
